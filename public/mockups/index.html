These are interactive HTML mockups designed to showcase three different branding approaches for Sip n Play. Each design:

  • Features the Sip n Play logo prominently

üé® Design Philosophy

Design 1 (Bold & Playful): For maximum brand presence and engagement. Great for standing out in app stores or as a hero page.

Design 2 (Clean & Modern): For a professional, approachable vibe. Works well if you want the focus on the game collection rather than branding.

Design 3 (Fun & Gaming): Perfect if you want to emphasize the playful, social nature of gaming at a cafe.
